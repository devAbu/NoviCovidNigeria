About us

Covid-19 Connect NG is a Pan African group volunteering to help reduce the risk and spread of covid-19 through proper sensitization and awareness. We have over 250 volunteers currently (and we are growing) who have been trained on the World Health Organization Operational Guidelines for Country Level Covid-19 Preparedness and Response.
